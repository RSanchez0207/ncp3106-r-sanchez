1. Attack on Titan

It is set in a world where humanity lives within cities surrounded by enormous walls that protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids referred to as Titans; the story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to retake the world after a Titan brings about the destruction of his home town and the death of his mother.

2. Highschool DxD

The story centers on Issei Hyodo, a high school student from Kuoh Academy who desires to be a harem king and is killed by his first date, revealed to be a fallen angel, but is later revived as a devil by Rias Gremory to serve her and her devil family.

3. HunterXHunter

The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss who discovers that his father, who left him at a young age, is actually a world renowned Hunter, a licensed professional who specializes in fantastical pursuits. Despite being abandoned by his father, Gon departs on a journey to become a Hunter and eventually find his father.

4. Your Lie In April

Arima Kouse is a piano prodigy that is unable to play music after his mother dies, until he meets a violin player. Miyazono Kaori helps Kousei return to the music world and show that it should be free and mold breaking unlike the structured and rigid style Kousei was used to.

5. Steins; Gate

Okabe Rintarou, a university student who refers to himself as Crazy Mad Scientist Hououin Kyouma and his lab's members work on a microwave device that can transfer messages to the past. Without getting captured, they should get it working in order to beat the evil organization, SERN and stop their evil plans.

6. Full Metal Alchemist: Brotherhood

The Seven Deadly Sins are a band of knights in the land of Britannia who had disbanded ten years earlier after being framed for plotting a coup of the Liones Kingdom, the Holy Knights who sequestered them before taking control in the wake of a rebellion they organized.

7. Demon Slayer

A family is attacked by demons and only two members survive - Tanjiro and his sister Nezuko, who is turning into a demon slowly. Tanjiro sets out to become a demon slayer to avenge his family and cure his sister. ... Demon Slayer focuses on Tanjirou Kamado, who is still very young, but is the only man in his family.

8. Kuroko no Basuke

The Teiko Middle School Basketball Team. The school that produced three perfect seasons in a row, with five once-in-a generation players, called "The Generation of Miracles." There was another player who all of them respected... A legendary 6th player. An up-and-coming power player, Taiga Kagami, is just back from America.

9. Assassination Classroom

Students must kill an octopus-like alien who portrays a teacher. Otherwise, he will destroy the Earth once the school year is over.

10. Noragami

Yato is a minor deity who lacks even a single shrine. Yato also finds a boy named Yukine who becomes a "sacred treasure" that can be used by a god. ... The three of them battle "you" who bring harm to humans as Yato's hidden history is gradually revealed.
